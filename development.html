General Concept


This project will follow the sun. Solar panel has 2 axis of rotation from East to West and South to North depending on the sun position and at 00:00 AM will be reseted at the rising position.

Block Diagram


Inputs are from light sensors, motor position and RTC. Current sensor it's for protection. Outputs are the 2 servo motors and blinking the LEDs.
